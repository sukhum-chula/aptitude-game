Bargain Blitz

Mental Math · Percentage Estimation · Speed

A 4×5 shelf is stocked with marked-down items. Every round picks a new discount threshold — your job is to grab only the deals that actually beat it. Misjudge the percentage and the wrong item lands in your Returns pile. Five rounds, five-minute clock.

How to Play

  • Read the banner. Each round shows the threshold — e.g. "under 25% of full price."
  • Pick the deals. Click items whose red price beats the threshold. Correct picks slide to Cart; wrong picks slide to Returns.
  • Score. Correct +1, wrong picks cost nothing — they just clutter your Returns.
  • Tap NEXT ROUND any time to skip remaining items and load a fresh shelf.
  • 5 rounds, 5-minute clock. Round 1 is gentle (50%); rounds 2–5 tighten to 20% or 25%.
