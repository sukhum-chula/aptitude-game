Flow Forge

Spatial Reasoning · Logical Planning · Pathfinding

Rotate pipe tiles to route water from the source on the left to every open outlet on the right — without hitting any sealed valve. Three rounds of growing scope, one 5-minute clock.

How to Play

  • Click a pipe to rotate it 90° clockwise.
  • Click LAUNCH when the layout looks right — the water flows once and the round is decided.
  • Reach every open outlet (✓) to win. Hitting a sealed valve (🛑) loses the round.
  • Round 1: 4×4, one outlet. Round 2: 4×4, two outlets (T-junctions appear). Round 3: 5×5, two outlets.
  • Watch the 5:00 clock. When it hits 0:00 the in-progress round auto-launches with whatever you've built.
