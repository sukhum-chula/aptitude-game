Numeric Cascade

Visual Search · Speed · Focus · Working Memory

A fast 5×5 number-grid challenge inspired by the Schulte Table cognitive test. Find the next number in the sequence as quickly as you can — every correct tap cascades a new number into its place. Three rounds, one 5-minute clock.

How to Play

  • Tap numbers in order. Round 1 climbs 1 → 50, Round 2 descends 50 → 1, Round 3 climbs 1 → 50 again.
  • Cascade refill. Each correct tap fades out and a new number streams into the same cell — the grid is always 25 numbers around your current target.
  • Skip penalty. Tap a number too far ahead and every number you skipped is wiped from the grid. They no longer count toward your score.
  • Watch the clock. One 5:00 countdown across all three rounds. Hit 0:00 and the run ends, finished or not.
  • Shortcuts. P pauses · R resets (with confirm).
