Phantom Path

Pattern Recognition · Forecasting · Visual Tracking

A phantom drifts down a 10×10 grid one row per second, weaving through a hidden anchor pattern. After it falls off the bottom, you have three seconds to predict where it will land on the unseen row 11. Decode the pattern, beat the fading trail, and rack up a streak before the 3-minute clock runs out.

How to Play

  • Watch the descent. Every second the phantom moves one row down. Its column wobbles around a hidden anchor that drifts ±1 every 4 steps.
  • Predict row 11. When the phantom exits the board, click the column where you think it will land next. Take as long as you need.
  • Mind the fading trail. Each visited cell glows for 10 seconds, then disappears. By the time you're predicting, the earliest dots may be gone.
  • Streaks & speed. +1 per correct guess. +1 extra for every consecutive correct beyond two. +1 lightning bonus if you click within the first second.
